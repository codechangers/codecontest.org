About

Code Contest

is a competitive, one-day event for kids 10-14 interested in creating their own .io video game and learning to code!

Win amazing prizes: from local coupons to Nintendo Switches!

Work with a team of up to two, or create your own!

Hosted by corporations, companies, districts, schools, and cities

Supported by local donations and companies

Created by the leading minds in tech education to give kids a memorable experience and an exciting introduction to coding.
